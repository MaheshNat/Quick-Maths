Number Sense Test

The Number Sense Test enables you to practice specific number sense skills, at varying lengths of time in order to prepare you for the real number sense test.

To submit your final answer to a question. Hit enter on your keyboard. If you want to skip a question, just hit enter to move on.

Repeating decimals are represented by parenthesis (), and the repeating elements of a decimal will be enclosed in parenthesis in both questions and answer. Example: .2(35) = .2353535...

Only scores submitted with the default settings (all problems included, 600 seconds) will count towards the Number Sense Leaderboard.
